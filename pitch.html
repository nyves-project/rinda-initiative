⚙️ How It Works

From first touch to test result in 48 hours

1

Youth discovers Rinda — in any way

Via the web platform on a smartphone, OR by dialling *775# on any basic phone, OR from a peer agent at school, clinic, or community event. No internet required for USSD.

2

Takes a quick Risk Assessment (3 minutes)

10 anonymous questions generate a personalised HIV risk score. Low/Medium/High with instant, non-judgmental action steps. All data stays on-device — nothing stored server-side.

3

Orders a self-test kit anonymously

No name, no ID, no clinic visit needed. Selects their sector. A trained peer agent packs the kit in plain packaging and delivers within 24–48 hours — a discreet knock at the door.

4

Gets result + learns what to do next

The Knowledge Hub explains exactly how to read results. If positive, the app connects to the free 114 counselling line. If negative, sets a 6-month testing reminder automatically.

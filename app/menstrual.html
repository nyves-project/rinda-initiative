💡 Health Tip

📦 Order Free Reusable Pads

Locate the nearest youth center distributing free reusable pads, or order them to be delivered quietly.

🧼 Hygiene & Washing Guide

1
Soak in cold water: Always soak your reusable pad in cold water first. Hot water sets blood stains.
2
Wash with soap: Use regular body soap or washing soap. Gently rub the fabric together until clean.
3
Dry in direct sunlight: This is critical. Direct sunlight kills bacteria. Do not dry in damp, hidden areas.
4
Store safely: Keep in a clean, dry bag ready for next month.

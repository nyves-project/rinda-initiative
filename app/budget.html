🗓️ 6-Month Timeline

Pilot Execution Roadmap

M1
Month 1 — Setup & Training

Foundation

Recruit and train 30 peer educators in 2 districts. Integrate live *775# USSD gateway. Establish baseline testing rate data.

M2
Month 2 — Soft Launch

First Deliveries

Begin anonymous kit deliveries and USSD service. School awareness events. Monitor first 100 orders and gather user feedback.

M3
Month 3 — Midterm Review

Evaluation & Adjustment

Midline survey. Evaluate USSD adoption. Adjust delivery schedules based on demand. Communities of >50 active USSD users identified for deeper engagement.

M4
Month 4 — Scale Within Districts

Expanding Reach

Activate negotiation audio modules with school counsellors. Peer agents conduct community health fairs. 200+ kits delivered to date.

M5
Month 5 — Consolidation

Community Adoption

Peer-to-peer referrals growing organically. Disabilities inclusion: sign-language agents fully deployed. Endline data collection begins.

M6
Month 6 — Endline & Scale Proposal

Evidence & Expansion

Complete endline evaluation. Present impact data to Imbuto Foundation for Phase 2 nationwide scaling. All 500 kits distributed.
